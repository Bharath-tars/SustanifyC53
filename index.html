Problem Statement


Biodiversity conservation is a critical environmental issue that requires urgent attention. With the increasing human population and changing land use patterns, ecosystems are being degraded at an alarming rate, leading to the loss of biodiversity. The challenge lies in identifying areas of high biodiversity and prioritizing conservation efforts to protect endangered species and ecosystems. While there are various tools and methods available to achieve this, the lack of a comprehensive and integrated approach to biodiversity conservation remains a major barrier.
